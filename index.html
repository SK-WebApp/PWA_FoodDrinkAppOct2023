Hello, thank you for visiting.

Do you have difficulty chewing and swallowing your favorite food or coughing up your favorite drink? People who have strokes and/or other neurological problems often experience such issues.

How to maintain good airway safety while eating and drinking?
Follow the steps outlined on this app and it will help determine whether your drink or food is safe to consume, according to International Dysphagia Diet Standardisation Initiative (IDDSI) standards. This app also provides recommendations on how to modify your drink or food for safer consumption.

Remember to always consult with your doctor and speech-language pathologist if you're experiencing difficulty with swallowing.

Notes on App Navigation:
Click on the Test App button below or at the top to begin exploring the application. Please use the built-in buttons to navigate between pages instead of the browser's forward and back arrow buttons.
